Sales & Order Performance Analysis (Excel)

The main objective of this project is to analyze sales and order performance to understand customer behavior, channel performance, gender-wise sales contribution, and city-wise revenue distribution using Excel.

Key Insights

  • Women customers contribute significantly higher sales compared to men across all age groups.
  • Amazon is the top-performing sales channel, contributing the highest share of total orders, followed by Myntra and Flipkart.
  • Adult age group dominates orders, indicating strong purchasing power among working professionals.
  • Bengaluru and Hyderabad are the top revenue-generating cities, showing high market demand in metro areas.
